Pricing update

A couple of weeks ago we announced our pricing.

Mixture has a range of users and use cases and we’ve always known that we were never going to please everyone.

Following our pricing announcement we received a lot of feedback on Twitter and email from users asking for a one-time price option.

Today we are adjusting the pricing to better accommodate these users.

Due to these changes we are extending the beta by a few days, this pricing will now come into place on the 9th of September:

One-time cost → $39 (£25.16)
  • The Mac & Windows application ¹
  • All updates (major new versions may be subject to an upgrade fee).

…or…

Monthly subscription → $12.99 (£8.40)
  • The Mac & Windows application (free) ¹
  • Unlimited hosting + publishing (including custom domains) ²
  • All updates including major versions.
14 day free trial
  • New users will benefit from 14 day trial of the application and all online services ³

The above only details an overview, Mixture is full of features — for the full details across the application and online services, please see the home page and docs.

¹ The application comes with a 2 machine licence and these are editable through your online account.

² All online services will be subject to our fair usage policy.

³ All pricing and plans will take effect from the 9th of September, the 14 day trial will be available to new users from the 9th. New and current users will be prompted to purchase the application once their trial period is over. If you choose to cancel your subscription any hosted / published projects associated with your account will be inaccessible.
