Beta no more

We first introduced Mixture back in September 2012, today we’re really pleased to announce that we move out of Beta!

If you tried Mixture early on, it’s definitely worth having another look as it’s changed massively over the last few months, if you’ve never tried it – well, what are you waiting for?

Today we bring in pricing, new features, fixes and a whole new online admin. As well as allowing you to purchase and manage your order(s) and the users associated with these orders, we’ve brought in a couple of highly requested features that we’ve had planned from the start.

Collaborators

We always recommend using version control like Git, especially when working in teams, Mixture projects can be fully version controlled.

But when working with a team of people the online side of Mixture is super useful - one pain point has always been that only the owner of the project could publish it…

You can now add collaborators to your published projects, by doing this you give those users access to publish on your behalf.

Note: you will need to be a subscriber to publish.

Boilerplates

Boilerplates have always been a key feature of Mixture - now you can add and share your own!

You can add private boilerplates or make them public and share with all users - go on, share your perfect starting point!

If you have an account you can check out the new admin here https://accounts.mixture.io.

If you’ve never used Mixture, simply download the app and follow the steps to setup your account, as a new user you’ll automatically benefit from a 14 day trial of the application and all online services.

Please keep in mind that we’ve made some big changes today to the apps, online and DNS settings - please be patient with us, if you have any issues contact us via email and we’ll do our best to resolve quickly.

Finally - thank you very much to everyone who has used and supported Mixture.
